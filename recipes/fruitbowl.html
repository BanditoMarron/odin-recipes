Fruit bowl

Food of the Gods

Fruit bowls are easy to make and even easier to make look impressive!

Follow our steps below and you'll seem a lot more savvy in the kitchen than you actually are!

What you'll need

  • Bananas
  • Red and green grapes
  • Strawberries
  • Mixed nuts
  • Raisins
  • Granola
  • Yogurt

The right steps

  1. Cut up your bananas and strawberries

    2. Into quarters or halves!

  2. Slice up your bananas

    3. It's super easy!

  3. Throw all of your fruit into a bowl

    4. Like... really easy!

  4. Throw some granola on top

    5. Yeah!

  5. Plop on a big spoonful of yogurt

    6. BAM! lol!

  6. Sprinkle your mixed nuts, raisins, and some more granola on top

    7. It makes it look cool!

  7. Put a spoon to the side

    8. Or not! Either way it's instagram ready!
